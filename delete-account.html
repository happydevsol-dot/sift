Sift Account and Data Deletion

Effective date: May 9, 2026

This page explains how users of Sift, operated by Happy Dev Sol, can request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

How to request account deletion

To request deletion of your Sift account and associated personal data, email us at:

happy.dev.sol@gmail.com

Please use the subject line: Delete my Sift account.

What to include in your request

To help us process your request, include the email address or account identifier associated with your Sift account. We may ask you to verify ownership of the account before deleting data.

Data deleted

When your deletion request is verified, we will delete or anonymize personal data associated with your account, which may include:

  • Account information, such as email address, display name, and account status
  • Authentication/session data associated with your account
  • Saved stories, saved articles, followed topics, preferences, and personalization data
  • Push notification tokens linked to your account or device
  • Support messages associated with your deletion request, where deletion is legally permitted

Data that may be retained

We may retain limited information where necessary for legal, security, fraud-prevention, accounting, audit, dispute-resolution, or legitimate business purposes. We may also retain anonymized or aggregated data that can no longer reasonably identify you.

Retention period

Verified deletion requests are processed within a reasonable period. Some backup, security, or log data may remain for a limited retention period before being automatically deleted or anonymized.

If you do not have a Sift account

If you use Sift without creating an account, there may be no account for us to delete. You may still contact us to request deletion of personal data that may be associated with your device, support messages, or app usage, where applicable and technically possible.

Contact

For account or data deletion requests, contact: happy.dev.sol@gmail.com
