Privacy Policy for Sift

Effective date: May 9, 2026

Sift (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we share it, how long we keep it, and the choices you have when using the Sift mobile app, website, and related services.

If you have any questions, contact us at happy.dev.sol@gmail.com.

The information we collect depends on how you use Sift and which features are enabled. Some features may not be available to all users.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information, such as your email address, display name, password hash, and account status.
  • Authentication information, such as session tokens and refresh tokens.
  • Preferences and personalization data, such as chosen topics, regions, language, followed sources, and saved stories or articles.
  • Content interactions, such as stories and articles you save, follow, search for, view, or interact with.
  • Device and technical information, such as device identifiers, push notification tokens, IP address, app version, operating system, crash reports, diagnostic data, and log data.
  • Communication information, such as messages or requests you send to support.
  • Usage and analytics information, such as feature usage, app interactions, performance data, and error events.

We may also collect information automatically from your device and browser when you use our services.

2. Information You Provide Directly

You may provide information to us when you:

  • Create an account
  • Verify your email address
  • Log in
  • Save stories or articles
  • Set preferences
  • Enable push notifications
  • Contact support
  • Use B2B, developer, or API features, if applicable

3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain Sift
  • Authenticate users and manage accounts
  • Save your preferences and content choices
  • Deliver news stories, article details, and search results
  • Personalize your experience
  • Send push notifications and account emails
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Debug, secure, monitor, and improve the service
  • Generate story summaries, clustering, search features, and other automated news analysis
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Automated Processing and AI Features

Sift uses automated systems, including self-hosted AI models, to process publicly available news article content. These systems help generate features such as story clustering, summaries, topic enrichment, claim extraction, search interpretation, and related news analysis.

Sift may use your searches, preferences, saved content, and interaction history to improve your personal app experience, such as ranking stories or remembering your settings.

We do not use your private account information, saved stories, personal preferences, or support messages to train public third-party AI models.

5. Advertising

Sift may display ads in the app. Advertising partners may collect or receive device information, advertising identifiers, approximate location, usage data, and interaction data to deliver, measure, prevent fraud, and improve ads.

Where required, we will request consent or provide choices for personalized advertising. You may also be able to limit ad personalization through your device or Google account settings.

6. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information with:

  • Service providers that help us host, store, secure, monitor, or operate the service
  • Email and notification providers
  • Analytics, logging, and crash-reporting providers
  • Advertising partners, if ads are enabled
  • Infrastructure providers such as hosting, database, cache, and search providers
  • Legal authorities when required by law or to protect rights, safety, or security

We may also share information in connection with a business transaction such as a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or asset sale.

7. Data Retention

We keep personal information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, including:

  • Keeping your account active
  • Preserving saved content and preferences
  • Operating the service
  • Meeting legal, tax, security, and audit obligations

When you delete your account or request deletion, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within a reasonable period, unless we are required to keep certain data for legal, security, fraud-prevention, accounting, or legitimate business purposes.

8. Account and Data Deletion

If you have a Sift account, you may request deletion of your account and associated personal data by emailing happy.dev.sol@gmail.com with the subject line “Delete my Sift account.”

We may ask you to verify ownership of the account before processing the request. After deletion, we may retain limited information where necessary for legal, security, fraud-prevention, accounting, or legitimate business purposes.

9. Your Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your personal information
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Request a copy of your data

You can make a request by emailing happy.dev.sol@gmail.com. You can also manage some information in the app itself, such as saved stories, saved articles, followed content, and notification settings.

10. Push Notifications

If you enable push notifications, we may collect and store a push token for your device so we can send notifications. You can disable push notifications in your device settings or in the app.

11. Cookies and Similar Technologies

If you use our website, we may use cookies or similar technologies for authentication, preferences, security, analytics, advertising, and basic functionality.

12. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

13. Children’s Privacy

Sift is not directed to children under 13, or the minimum age required by applicable law in your country. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children below that age. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us at happy.dev.sol@gmail.com.

14. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, including by service providers and infrastructure providers. These countries may have data protection laws that are different from those in your jurisdiction.

15. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will post the updated version and revise the effective date above.
